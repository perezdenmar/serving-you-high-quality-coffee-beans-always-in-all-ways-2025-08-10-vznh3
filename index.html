About Cofface

Founded in 2020, Cofface began with a simple mission: to bring exceptional coffee to discerning coffee lovers. Our journey started in a small roastery in Manila, where our passion for perfect roasts and sustainable practices laid the foundation for what we've become today. Through years of dedication and expertise, we've built strong relationships with coffee farmers worldwide and developed our signature roasting processes.

Our mission remains unchanged: to deliver the finest coffee beans while supporting sustainable farming practices and fair trade partnerships. We believe in transparency, quality, and environmental responsibility. Every bean we source, every batch we roast, and every bag we ship reflects our commitment to excellence and sustainability in the coffee industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your coffee beans special?

Our beans are carefully selected from the top 1% of coffee harvests worldwide. We maintain direct relationships with farmers, ensuring sustainable practices and fair compensation. Each batch is roasted to perfection using our proprietary process that brings out the unique flavor profile of each origin.

How do you ensure freshness?

We roast in small batches weekly and ship within 24 hours of roasting. Each bag is equipped with a one-way valve to prevent oxidation and includes the roast date. We recommend consuming our coffee within 30 days of roasting for optimal flavor.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on orders over $50 within the Philippines. International shipping is available to select countries. All orders are processed within 24 hours and typically arrive within 2-5 business days, depending on your location.

Do you offer subscriptions?

Yes! Our subscription service ensures you never run out of fresh coffee. Choose your preferred beans, frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly), and quantity. Subscribers enjoy a 10% discount and free shipping on all orders.

What is your return policy?

We stand behind our products 100%. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 14 days of delivery for a full refund or replacement. We'll even cover the return shipping costs.
