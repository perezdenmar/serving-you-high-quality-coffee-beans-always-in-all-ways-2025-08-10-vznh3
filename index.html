Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Cofface coffee unique?

Our premium single-origin Arabica beans come from our own 1-hectare farm in Davao del Sur, Philippines. With complete value chain control from cultivation to packaging, we ensure unmatched quality and full traceability. Our innovative packaging uses safe engineered bamboo and bagasse alternatives, prioritizing both sustainability and consumer health.

How do you ensure coffee quality and safety?

We control every step from farm to cup: cultivation, pulping, hulling, drying, roasting, grinding, and packaging. Our rigorous quality control includes testing for both coffee excellence and packaging safety. We avoid harmful chemicals like melamine-formaldehyde resins in our packaging materials.

What are your sustainable practices?

We use precision agriculture techniques and sustainable farming practices on our Davao del Sur farm. Our packaging innovation focuses on engineered bamboo (without harmful resins) and bagasse alternatives. We're committed to environmental responsibility while maintaining premium quality standards.

How can I purchase Cofface coffee?

You can order directly through our website or contact us at coffaceofficial@gmail.com. We offer both direct sales to consumers and B2B partnerships with specialty coffee shops and cafes. We accept cash, bank transfer, and digital payments with wholesale terms available for businesses.

What are your expansion plans?

We're targeting a 300% production increase through scientific farming methods and precision agriculture. Our goal is ₱2-3 million revenue in Year 1 with expanded farm operations, while maintaining our commitment to quality and sustainability. We're also developing partnerships with certified suppliers and distributors.
